Bar Info

Black Rabbit Saloon

Located at 127 E Hopkins, Black Rabbit Saloon offers a wide variety of cocktails, beers on tap and a food menu. They also have entertaiment such as skee-ball and other games. Open from 5-2 daily

Chances R

Located at 138 S LBJ, Chances R is a quiet, cozy bar with a jukebox and pool tables. The perfect place to relax without the hectic bar scene. Open from 3-2 daily

Chimy's

Located at 217 E Hopkins, Chimy's is a San Marcos treasure. Serving classic margaritas and draft beers as well as a full menu of mexican cuisine. Open from 11-Midnight daily. Closed Sunday

Crafthouse

Located at 242 N LBJ, Crafthouse is known for their craft beers on tap, live music and pub food. Don't want a beer? Don't worry, go next door to the Daquiri Factory and grab a delicious Daquiri. Open from 11-Midnight daily

Green Parrot

Located at 124 N LBJ, Green Parrot is your old fashoned bar with alot of character. A great place to grab some cheap drinks. Open from 5-2 daily

Harpers & Veranda

Located at 139 E Hopkins, Harpers is your classic sports bar with plenty of TVs to catch your favorite game. Veranda is located on the rooftop of Harpers with a full bar and an amazing view of San Marcos. Open from 4-2 daily

Louie's Beer Garden

Located at 119 E Huchison just off the Square, Louie's offers a wide selection of beers on tap and serves amazing seafood along with it. Open from 12-Midnight daily

Mayloo's

Located at 138 N LBJ, Mayloo's is one of the classier bars in San Marcos, offering a wide cocktail selection and a classy atmosphere. Open from 4-2 daily

Nephews

Located at 100 N Guadalupe, Nephews is know for their nightclub atmoshphere. With 2 dancefloors, it's the perfect place to dance with your friends. Open from 8-2, closed Sun, Mon, Wed

Rooftop

Located at 126 S Guadalupe, Rooftop offers a wide variety of cocktails and shots with a rooftop view of the Square. Open from 10-2 daily

Sean Patricks

Located at 202 E San Antonio, Sean Patricks Irish Pub offers classic pub food with a selection of beer and pub games. Open from 11-1 daily

Shade

Located at 127 E Hopkins, Shade Rooftop Patio Bar offers a calm outdoor atmoshphere with a selection of beers and cocktails. Open from 5-2 daily

Taproom

Located at 129 E Hopkins, Taproom is a classic bar with a wide selection of drinks and American pub food. Open from 12-1 daily

The Marc

Located at 120 E San Antonio, The Marc is another San Marcos treasure. With its huge dancefloor and multiple bars, The Marc is the best place to dance with your friends. Open from 10-2. Closed Sunday & Monday

The Porch

Located at 129 E Hopkins, The Porch offers a relaxing bar scene with TVs and food. Sit at the bar or relax on the outdoor section with couches and swings. Open from 12-2 daily

The Vault

Located at 100 W Hopkins, The Vault is a classic nightclub with a large dancefloor and a sake bar attached. Open from 7-2 daily. Closed Sunday

Vodka Street

Located at 202 N LBJ, Vodka Street offers a wide range of food and a full service bar. Open from 8-2 daily
